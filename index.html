Receipt Itemizer

Extract, track, and categorize individual line items on paper receipts.

Why another receipt scanning app?

Most receipt scanning apps only extract totals.

Receipt Itemizer automatically extracts individual line items providing greater insight into your spending habits.

How does it work?

  1. Take a picture of your receipt
  2. Verify the information is correct
  3. Create some tags
  4. Tag receipt items
  5. Set a monthly budget goal, and make sure your on track
